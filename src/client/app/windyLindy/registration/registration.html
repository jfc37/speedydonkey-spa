Full Swing Ltd. reserves the right to make changes to the schedule of any events that are part of Windy Lindy 2015 at anytime owing to factors beyond our control or to ensure that the event runs smoothly. In the event of major schedule changes or cancelations, all effort will be made to notify participants as soon possible, and/or offer an alternative activity.

Swing dance of all kinds is a physical activity. By registering for Windy Lindy 2015, registrants accept personal responsibility for their health and wellbeing while participating in all parts of the event. This includes assessing their own ability to complete activities, and behaving in a way that is safe for themselves and other registrants.

The Windy Lindy 2015 team reserves the right to remove any registrant from classes, social events or any part of Windy Lindy 2015 in cases of inappropriate behaviour. Please refer to the Full Swing Code of Conduct for more details.
